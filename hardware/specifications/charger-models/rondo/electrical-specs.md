# Rondo Charger - Electrical Specifications

## Overview

The Rondo is BitRocket's premium EV charging station designed for high-performance commercial and residential use.

## General Specifications

- **Model:** BR-RONDO-350
- **Input Voltage:** 3-phase 400V AC ±10%
- **Input Frequency:** 50/60 Hz ±5%
- **Maximum Output Power:** 350 kW
- **Output Voltage Range:** 100-1000V DC
- **Output Current Range:** 0-500A DC
- **Efficiency:** >95%
- **Power Factor:** >0.99 at full load

## Connector Specifications

### Main Connector (CCS Type 2)
- **Standard:** IEC 62196-3 Type 2
- **Current Rating:** 500A DC
- **Voltage Rating:** 1000V DC
- **Cable Length:** 5 meters
- **Cable Diameter:** 12 mm²

### Optional Connectors
- CHAdeMO
- Tesla Supercharger
- GB/T (Chinese standard)

## Charging Profiles

### Fast Charging
- **0-80% SOC:** 15 minutes (for 200 kWh battery)
- **0-100% SOC:** 30 minutes (for 200 kWh battery)

### Standard Charging
- **Power Levels:** 50 kW, 100 kW, 150 kW, 250 kW, 350 kW
- **Adjustable Current:** 0-500A in 1A increments

## Safety Features

- **Overvoltage Protection:** ±15% of rated voltage
- **Overcurrent Protection:** 120% of rated current
- **Overheating Protection:** Thermistor-based temperature monitoring
- **Ground Fault Protection:** 30 mA residual current device (RCD)
- **Surge Protection:** IEC 61000-4-5 compliant
- **Short Circuit Protection:** Magnetic circuit breaker

## Environmental Ratings

- **Operating Temperature:** -30°C to +50°C
- **Storage Temperature:** -40°C to +70°C
- **Humidity:** 5-95% (non-condensing)
- **Ingress Protection:** IP55
- **Vibration:** IEC 60068-2-64
- **Shock:** IEC 60068-2-27

## Compliance

- IEC 61851-1:2017 (Electric vehicle conductive charging system)
- IEC 61851-23:2014 (DC charging station)
- UL 2202:2016 (Safety of electric vehicle charging equipment)
- CE Marking
- FCC Part 15 Class A

## Dimensions and Weight

- **Height:** 2100 mm
- **Width:** 800 mm
- **Depth:** 600 mm
- **Weight:** 180 kg

## Communication Interfaces

- **Ethernet:** 10/100/1000 Mbps RJ45
- **Wi-Fi:** 802.11a/b/g/n/ac
- **Cellular:** 4G LTE, 5G ready
- **Bluetooth:** BLE 5.0
- **RS-485:** Modbus RTU
- **CAN Bus:** ISO 11898

## Energy Management

- **Load Balancing:** Dynamic load distribution between multiple chargers
- **Peak Shaving:** Optimized charging during off-peak hours
- **Solar Integration:** Direct DC coupling from solar panels
- **Battery Storage:** Support for bidirectional charging (V2G)

## Monitoring and Diagnostics

- **Real-time Monitoring:** Voltage, current, power, temperature
- **Fault Detection:** Automatic fault diagnosis and reporting
- **Remote Firmware Updates:** Over-the-air (OTA) updates
- **Data Logging:** 10 years of charging history
